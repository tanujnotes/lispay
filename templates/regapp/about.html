About Us




---------- OUR STORY ----------

We are a bunch of coders who are really fascinated by the creativity of people all over the world. This fascination and our interest in experimenting with things led us to question ourselves - can we help those creators in any possible way? Can we make it easier for them to do the stuff they love to do? We started by developing a simple Android app which made it easier for people to discover and watch YouTube videos from independent creators. While the app didn't take off, having closely followed those creators and talking to some of them, we realized that the problem is not what we thought it was.

If we ignore the few top players, for creators it's almost always hard to generate enough revenue to support themselves and their art. We always call them independent creators but are they truly independent? With this insight, we set out to solve the problem of generating sustainable and reliable revenue for independent creators. After much brainstorming and talking to a number of people, voluntary payment from supporters made much more sense than any other option. You may think about alternatives like making money from advertisements or paid promotions but the problem is that those revenue sources are never reliable. They promote quantity over quality and creators always have to make some compromises in their art. Not anymore. With the help of what we're building here, creators and their fans can now take matters in their own hands. We admit that this is not perfect solution but this does bring creators very close to being truly independent.

Some people may have doubts about what we're doing here but we don't. Do you know why? Whenever we asked a creator about who do they trust most to support them no matter what, "their fans" was the only answer we got.
