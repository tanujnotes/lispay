What is Lisplay?

Lisplay allows independent creators to get paid by running a membership program and connect with their fans. Register on Lisplay as a creator. After that your fans can join your membership program by paying a small monthly subscription fees. This way your fans will get a chance to support your work and you’ll have more time and resources to focus on the work you love to do instead of chasing advertisers and sponsors and worry about revenue all the time.


Why will anyone pay if my content is already free for everyone?

Why do your fans do anything that you request them to do? If they want, they can just watch your videos, listen to your music, read your blogs and just move on. But no. They take time to do things for your benefit because they believe that you deserve it. It's time that you start believing that too. Besides, you'll never know until and unless you try.


What do I need to do for my supporters on Lisplay?

Supporters are paying you voluntarily so rewarding them is not mandatory but it's highly recommended. You can reward them by thanking them on social media, providing them exclusive content and sneak peaks, chatting with and even meeting them in person if possible. You decide the rewards so it's totally up to you.


Do I need to post my content on Lisplay?

Not at all. Lisplay is a payment platform, not a social media platform. You keep posting your content on platform(s) of your choice. Lisplay will help you with running your membership program, handling payments and connecting with your supporters.


How do I get started on Lisplay as a creator?

Sign up on Lisplay and fill all the details required for a creator account. That's it. Once the registration process is complete, your fans can start paying you on Lisplay. Don't forget to share this good news with them.


How do earnings get distributed?

Creators get 90% of the total payment made to them. Transaction charges averages to 5% and we take the rest of the 5% as our fees. Please note that even if transaction charges are more of less than 5%, creators still get their 90%.


How do I get the money and when?

We start to transfer money to creators at end of each month and it takes about a week. We'll inform you in advance if there's any delay. To make sure that the money is going to the right person, we do a thorough verification of creators before we transfer them any money. We'll contact you for the verification process.

To keep receiving that content for free. Contrary to the popular belief, being a full time creator is not easy at all. How much do I have to pay?

Minimum amount that you can pay is Rs. 10 and maximum is Rs. 9999. Lisplay is a voluntary payment platform so you choose the amount you want to pay. Please note that you can cancel your subscription any time you want and we don't offer any refunds.
