What is Lisplay?

Lisplay is a monthly subscription based crowdfunding platform for independent creators. Through Lisplay, you can provide financial help to your favourite creators. Direct help from their fans means that creators won’t have to do jobs on the side or chase sponsors/advertisers. With your help they can keep doing what they love to do and you get to enjoy their creations and satisfaction of knowing that you made a difference.


What can I do on Lisplay?

If you love the work of some YouTubers, bloggers or simply any kind of creator, you can support their work financially through Lisplay in the form of a monthly subscription. Just find your favorite creators on Lisplay and subscribe to them.


What if my favourite creator in not on Lisplay?

Let them know through on social media that you'd like to support them on Lisplay. You can also tell us which creators you want on Lisplay and we'll do our best to invite them. You can contact us through social media or email support@lisplay.com


Why should I pay for content which I can access for free?

To keep receiving that content for free. Contrary to the popular belief, being a full time creator is not easy at all. Apart from being creative almost all the time, creators also have to worry about finances. Now, if their fans start to support them directly, they won't have to chase down advertisers and sponsors. They can simply be a full time creator, create the content you love and everyone lives happily ever after.


How do I start?

It's quite easy. Just create an account on Lisplay, search your favourite creator, go to their page follow the steps mentioned.


What payment options are supported?

For subscriptions on Lisplay, we are currently able to support payments only by credit cards. But more payment options are coming very soon. Promise.


Do I get anything in return for ?

Yes, of course! Apart from your own satisfaction, you get eternal gratitude from creators and entire Lisplay team. :D


Is there any refund option?

We have a strict NO REFUND policy. Once we receive the payment, we'll be unable to credit it back to you. However, you can cancel your subscriptions anytime you want.


What is Lisplay?

Lisplay is a monthly subscription based crowdfunding platform for independent creators. As a creator you can register on Lisplay and ask your fans to voluntarily pay you a monthly subscription. This way you’ll have more time and resources to focus on the work you love to do instead of chasing advertisers and sponsors and worry about revenue all the time.


Why will anyone pay for the content which is already free?

Why do your fans do anything that you request them to do? If they want, they can just watch your videos, listen to your music, read your blogs and just move on. But no. They take time to do things for your benefit because they believe that you deserve it. It's time that you start believing that too. Besides, we'll never know until and unless we try.


How do I get started on Lisplay as a creator?

Sign up on Lisplay and fill all the details required for a creator account. That's it. Now you can start requesting your fans to support you on Lisplay. We'll transfer your share of earnings at the end of each month. We also do verifications to make sure that the money is going to the right person.


How do I get the money and when?

We start to transfer money to creators at end of each month and it takes about a week. To make sure that the money is going to the right person, we do a thorough verification of creators before we transfer them any money. We'll contact you for the verification process.


What do I need to do for my supporters on Lisplay?

Supporters are paying you voluntarily so there's no mandatory service that you need to provide. But, we still suggest doing something nice for your supporters. You can start with showing your gratitude to individual supporters on social media. They'd most certainly like that.
